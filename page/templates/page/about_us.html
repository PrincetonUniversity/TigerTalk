Confused where to start?

  • Confused where to start?

  • From the homepage, you can search for any Princeton extracurricular club. On each club's page, you can browse info related to the club, read other students' reviews of the club, or read past interview experiences. If you're a club leader, you can customize and edit your club's page from the "My Clubs" tab (or from your club's page directly).

  • Don't see your club?

  • Club Talk currently only features all ODUS-recognized clubs (per their website). If you want your club to be added, email princetonclubtalk@gmail.com.

  • Questions or comments?

  • We love feedback! If you found a bug or would like a new feature to be added, please email djmajor@princeton.edu.

About Us

Princeton Club Talk was built as a final project for COS333: Advanced Programming Techniques in Spring 2018 taught by Brian Kernighan and Jérémie Lumbroso.


We would like to give a special thanks to our advisor, Jerry Wei, for the help, advice, and encouragement throughout the development process.


We love feedback! If you find any bugs, have any questions, or would like your non-ODUS club to be added, please contact us at princetonclubtalk@gmail.com and we'll get back to you straight away!
