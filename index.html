Extracurricular clubs and actitivities are a major part of each student’s life at Princeton, and they have ramifactions in one’s academic, intellectual, and social life. Yet, students often make decisions on which clubs to join (especially freshman year) that are based off of little knowledge but have a huge impact on their life in college. Princeton Club Talk will change this by being the first-ever platform for exploring and learning about Princeton extracurricular clubs. Through the website, students can leave and read about anonymous reviews and interview processes. In addition, each club has its own customizable page where it can attract and communicate directly with students.
